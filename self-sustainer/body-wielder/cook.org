** Bolognese
   - soffritto
     - onion
     - celery
     - carrot
   - ragu
     - tomato paste
     - wine
     - passata
     - milk
     - 1kg pork minced
   - recipe
     - onion celery carrot fry on oil
     - after put half of wine
     - then put in meat, then salt and pepper
     - after browned, and meat water gone, add second half of wine
     - then add tomato paste and passata
     - cook for 4 hours, add water when needed
     - at the end, add milk
